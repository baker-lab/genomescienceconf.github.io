Genome Science 2018 will be held at the University of Nottingham

Tuesday, 4th September - Thursday, 6th September

Genome Science started life in 2011 as the UK Next Generation Sequencing Meeting, hosted by the DeepSeq facility at the University of Nottingham. Since then, the conference has moved between different host universities and has been rebranded as the Genome Science meeting.

The 2018 conference will return to the University of Nottingham!
