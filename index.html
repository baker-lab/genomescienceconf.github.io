Genome Science 2019 will be held at the University of Edinburgh

Tuesday, 3rd September - Thursday, 5th September

Genome Science started life in 2010 as the UK Next Generation Sequencing Meeting, hosted by the DeepSeq facility at the University of Nottingham. Since then, the conference has moved between different host universities and has been rebranded as the Genome Science meeting.

The Genome Science conference brings together scientists from around the world to share ideas on the rapidly evolving field of high throughput sequencing technologies and -omics data analysis.

It's Coming Home! The 2018 conference will return to the University of Nottingham!
